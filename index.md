## About This Book

This book is not an introductory book and will not cover the basics of shader programming. Each chapter of this book contains a recipe for one particular effect, and you don't need to read the chapters in order. However, the more fundamental effects are covered first, and the effects in later chapters are built on those techniques and effects. So, if you don't have a favorite chapter to begin with, I recommend starting from Chapter 1 and moving forward from there.
I hope that you find this book informative and enjoyable to read.
